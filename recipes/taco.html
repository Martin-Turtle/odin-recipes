Taco

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Ground beef or chicken
  • 1 packet Taco seasoning
  • 8 Taco shells
  • 1 large Lettuce
  • 6 chopped Tomatoes
  • 250 grams Cheese
  • 500ml Sour cream
  • 2 Avocado or guacamole

    • Instructions

    1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
    2. Add the taco seasoning and water, and simmer for 5 minutes.
    3. Warm the taco shells in the oven or microwave.
    4. Fill each taco shell with the meat mixture.
    5. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and avocado or guacamole.
    6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Variations

  • Fish Tacos: Use grilled or fried fish instead of meat.
  • Vegetarian Tacos: Use grilled vegetables, beans, or tofu as a filling.
  • Breakfast Tacos: Fill with scrambled eggs, cheese, and salsa.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with a side of Mexican rice, refried beans, or a fresh salad.

Pair with a refreshing drink like horchata or a margarita.

Storage Instructions

Store leftover taco filling in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Reheat the filling in a skillet or microwave before serving again.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, marinate the meat in lime juice and spices before cooking.
  • Use fresh ingredients for the best taste, especially for toppings like lettuce and tomatoes.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as queso fresco or pepper jack, for added flavor.
